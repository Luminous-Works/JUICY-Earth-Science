## Overview

The JUICY Academy Earth Science curriculum teaches plate tectonics, meteorology, oceanography, and geohazard science through the geography of Jamaica and the wider Caribbean — one of the most geologically active and climatically vulnerable regions on Earth.

Jamaica sits on a transform fault boundary. The Caribbean Sea is a distinct oceanic plate. Hurricanes form, intensify, and make landfall here. This is not background context — it is the curriculum.
