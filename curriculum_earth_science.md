# JUICY EARTH SCIENCE
## Semester 1 & 2: Geology, weather, ocean systems, and Geography

### 1. Geology & Lithosphere
*   **Plate Tectonics**: Understanding the movement of Earth's crustal plates and its role in earthquake and volcanic activity (Crucial for Jamaica).
*   **Rock Cycle**: Formation of Igneous, Sedimentary, and Metamorphic rocks.
*   **Minerals & Ores**: Focus on Bauxite (Jamaican Aluminum source) and Iron Ore composition.

### 2. Meteorology & Atmosphere
*   **Weather Patterns**: High/Low pressure systems, trade winds, and hurricane formation in the Caribbean basin.
*   **Climate Change**: Monitoring global temperature shifts and their impact on local agriculture and sea levels.
*   **Atmospheric Layers**: Troposphere to Exosphere.

### 3. Oceanography & Hydrosphere
*   **Ocean Currents**: Global conveyor belt and its role in heat distribution.
*   **Marine Ecosystems**: Coral reef health monitoring and local fisheries management.
*   **Water Cycle**: Evaporation, transpiration, and groundwater aquifer recharge.

### 4. Space & Geography
*   **Earth's Position**: Seasonal shifts and solar exposure.
*   **GIS Mapping**: Using coordinates (Lat/Lon) to track environmental changes.
